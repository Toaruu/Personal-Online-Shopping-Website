ALIENS X ALIEN STOMPER MID POWERLOADER 'THE FINAL SCENE PACK'

To celebrate the Alien franchise and Alien Day on April 26, 2017, Reebok released a two-shoe pack inspired by
the final battle scene in James Cameron’s 1986 movie Aliens. The ‘Queen’ Alien Stomper Mid is inspired by
the slimy black look of the Xenomorph XX121, sporting a black patent leather upper and a glow-in-the-dark midsole. The shoe also features ‘XX121’ branding on the bottom strap. The ‘Powerloader’
is based on the forklift-like exoskeleton that Ripley uses to fight the queen. It features a yellow leather upper with black stripes based
on the arms of the machine and a mesh panel overlay based on the net canopy on the machine’s roof. The shoe is finished off with an icy orange outsole underfoot
inspired by the power loader’s orange siren light.
